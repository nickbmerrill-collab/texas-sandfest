Frequently Asked Questions * These FAQ responses are subject to change. ​ 🐾 Pet Policy - Updated What is the Pet Policy for 2026? Texas SandFest is now a Service Animals Only event. Only Service Animals, as defined by the ADA, are permitted within the festival grounds. ​ Are Emotional Support Animals (ESAs) or therapy pets allowed? No. Per the ADA, the above do not qualify as Service Animals and will not be admitted. ​ What are the responsibilities of a Service Animal handler? A Service Animal must be under the control of its handler. Under the ADA, Service Animals must be harnessed, leashed, or tethered, unless the individual&rsquo;s disability prevents using these devices or these devices interfere with the Service Animal&rsquo;s safe, effective performance of tasks. In that case, the individual must maintain control of the animal through voice, signal, or other effective controls. Additionally: The animal must be housebroken and well-behaved. The handler is fully responsible for the animal&rsquo;s care, cleanup, and any damages caused. Can I still bring my pet to the beach outside the festival? Yes. This policy applies only to the Texas SandFest event grounds. ​ Where can I find more information on ADA requirements? For official federal guidance, visit the ADA website. 👜 Know Before You Go Can I bring a wagon to the event? Small children may be pulled in a wagon. ​ Are there any nearby hotels or accommodations? On the beach between Markers 13 and 17. North gate entrance: Marker 13. South gate entrance: Marker 17. Can we park our RV at Texas SandFest? Yes and no. Yes, outside the bollards south of Marker 19 (perpendicular parking only, max 3 nights). See City of Port Aransas RV parking regulations . Sand is held together by compaction and internal moisture. Different-sized grains fit together a lot like jigsaw puzzle pieces. ​ Why does the surface of the sculptures not wear down quickly? The sculptures' surfaces are sprayed with a proprietary, biodegradable, child- and sea-life-safe bonding agent. ​ Does rain damage the sculptures? The durability of a sculpture depends on the intensity of the rain: Light Rain: Generally has no impact and does not hurt the sculptures. Moderate Rain: May begin to soften or erase some of the finer details. Heavy Rain: Can cause significant damage to portions of the sculpture. Will wind damage the sculptures? Wind can damage the sculptures, especially the fine details. ​ What kind of sand is used? Only local beach sand is used. ​ How long do the sculptors in the Gallery have to sculpt their art? That depends on which category they are in. Master: 36 Hours over 5 days Semi Pro: 26.75 Hours over 4 days. Advanced Amateur: 18 Hours over 3 days How much sand does each category of Sculptor get to create their master piece? Master Solo: 12 cubic yards* Master Duo: 24 cubic yards* Semi Pro: 9 cubic yards* Advanced Amateur: 6 cubic yards* *These are approximate amounts. ​ Who are the judges? Judging is performed by other sculptors from other categories. ​ What is the prize money for the winners? Over $22,000 in total prize money is awarded across the various competition categories and specialty awards. ​ Are the sculptors using drinking water (potable) for their sculptures? No. The water used for sculpting is non-potable and sourced from two separate sources: an irrigation well and a local pond with brackish water. We do not use treated drinking water for the construction of any of the sand art you see at the event. ​ How many professional sculptors are there worldwide? There are fewer than 500 professional sand sculptors worldwide. ​ How many years does it take to become a Master Sculptor? It takes roughly 10 years of sand sculpting experience to become a Master Sculptor. ​ What backgrounds do some of the sculptors have? Ice or wood carving.
