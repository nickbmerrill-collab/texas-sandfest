The Texas SandFest Mission The mission of Texas SandFest is to give back to the community by raising funds for local non-profit organizations and scholarships. ​ Our commitment to this community truly shines through at Texas SandFest. ​ In 2025, Texas SandFest contributed a total of $464,169 to the Port Aransas community. We are honored to report that $274,419 of those funds were awarded as scholarships, with the remaining $189,750 distributed among our community-driven initiatives and local charitable organizations. ​ This is part of our growing legacy of giving: since 2012, Texas SandFest's total contributions have surpassed $3.3 Million . This impressive figure includes over $1.4 Million dedicated to student scholarships and more than $1.9 Million supporting community-driven initiatives and local charitable organizations. ​ Texas SandFest Grant Application Thank you for your organization's interest in applying for a grant from Texas SandFest. Below are the rules: Applications will only be accepted through our online portal Limit of one application per organization Submission of an application does not guarantee funding Organization must be a 501c3 in good standing Additional information or clarification may be required, and we will notify you via email should this be necessary. Incomplete applications will not be accepted Deadline: The 2026 deadline has passed.
