Texas SandFest is fully committed to ADA accessibility. We&rsquo;re deeply grateful to our pet-loving community for understanding and supporting this step.&rdquo; Under the ADA, only dogs (any breed) or miniature horses that perform disability-related tasks qualify as Service Animals. Emotional support animals, therapy animals, comfort animals, pets, and all other animals do not qualify and will not be permitted. Handlers must keep Service Animals under control, ensure they are housebroken and well-behaved, and handle all care/cleanup.
