# Parking & Shuttles - Texas Sandfest

## Source

https://www.texassandfest.org/parking-shuttles

## Headings

- Parking &amp; Transportation
- Wondering about the best way to get around?
- Use the guide below to navigate the festival and find the most convenient parking for your needs.
- &nbsp;
- ​ 🅿️ Off-Site Parking &amp; Shuttle Options
- 🚌 Park &amp; Ride (FREE Shuttle)
- The Regional Transportation Authority (CCRTA), in conjunction with the City of Port A, provides this free shuttle.&nbsp;
- ℹ️ General Information:
- Operating Hours:&nbsp;
- Friday: 9:00 AM &ndash; 8:00 PM
- Saturday: 9:00 AM &ndash; 8:00 PM
- Sunday: 9:00 AM &ndash; 8:00 PM
- Pickup and Drop-off Locations:
- Roberts Point Park: Located near the ferry landing. (Free Parking)
- Port Aransas Community Park: 700 Clark Parkway. (Free Parking)
- Near the South Entrance of the event (end of Access Road 1A at the Beach)
- ​​
- 🛺 Gracie&#39;s Way
- For a quicker, &quot;island-style&quot; transfer directly to the South Entrance, utilize the Gracie&#39;s Way lot.​
- Gracie&#39;s Way (near the intersection of 11th St and Access 1A)​​​
- Cost:
- Both parking and golf cart shuttle service are free
- 📍 On the Beach Parking Areas
- 🏖️ General SandFest Parking Lot
- Located at the North end of the event grounds.
- Entrance: Beach Marker 9
- Exit: Marker 0 at Avenue G
- ⚠️ Rules:
- A valid city Beach Parking Permit is required.
- No RVs, trailers, or oversized vehicles.
- ♿ Accessible SandFest Parking
- Space is limited and available on a first-come, first-served basis.
- Located at the North entrance. Look for the &quot;Accessible Parking&quot; Feather Flag.
- Entrance: Marker 12.5
- Exit: Marker 11.5.
- Must display a valid state-issued accessible placard or accessible plate, or Disabled Veterans plate&nbsp;
- A parking attendant will be at the entrance to verify a valid state-issued accessible placard/plate
- A valid city Beach Parking Permit is required.​
- 🌟 VIP Parking
- ℹ️ General Information:​
- Located at the South entrance of the event. Look for the &quot;VIP Parking&quot; Feather Flag.
- Entrance: Marker 19.
- Exit: Marker 17.5 at Access Road 1A.
- A parking attendant will be at the entrance to verify a valid VIP Placard
- An official SandFest VIP placard must be displayed on the dashboard
- A city Beach Parking Permit is also required.
- 📜 Beach Parking Permits
- Beach parking permits ($12) are valid for the calendar year and must be visibly displayed on all motorized vehicles (including golf carts) parked on the beach.
- Where to Purchase:
- The Texan or Stripes Convenience Stores
- Lowes Market (formerly IGA) &ndash; 418 S. Alister St.
- Tourism Bureau &amp; Chamber Welcome Center &ndash; 403 W. Cotter Ave.
- ⚠️ Important Parking Notes:
- Golf Carts: Only official SandFest carts are allowed inside the gates. Personal/rental carts must park in the designated beach lots.
- Enforcement: All beach lots are regularly patrolled by city parking enforcement.
- Free Parking: Extremely limited free spots are available between the South Jetty and Horace Caldwell Pier. These fill up very early; purchasing a permit is highly recommended.
