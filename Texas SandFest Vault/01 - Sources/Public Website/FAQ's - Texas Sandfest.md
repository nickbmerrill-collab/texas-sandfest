- What is the Pet Policy for 2026?
- Texas SandFest is now a Service Animals Only event. Only Service Animals, as defined by the ADA, are permitted within the festival grounds.
- ​
- Are Emotional Support Animals (ESAs) or therapy pets allowed?
- No. Per the ADA, the above do not qualify as Service Animals and will not be admitted.
- What are the responsibilities of a Service Animal handler?
- A Service Animal must be under the control of its handler. Under the ADA, Service Animals must be harnessed, leashed, or tethered, unless the individual&rsquo;s disability prevents using these devices or these devices interfere with the Service Animal&rsquo;s safe, effective performance of tasks. In that case, the individual must maintain control of the animal through voice, signal, or other effective controls.
- Additionally:
- The animal must be housebroken and well-behaved.
- The handler is fully responsible for the animal&rsquo;s care, cleanup, and any damages caused.
- Can I still bring my pet to the beach outside the festival?
- Yes. This policy applies only to the Texas SandFest event grounds.&nbsp;
- Where can I find more information on ADA requirements?
- For official federal guidance, visit the ADA website.

This applies to any media now known or developed in the future, worldwide, and in perpetuity&mdash;for promotional, commercial, news, or other purposes.
- Your presence constitutes consent; no individual releases are needed. By entering, you also waive claims related to compensation, approval rights, privacy, publicity, or similar issues.
- Even if you are just watching the sculptors build or walking through the area, you are included. ​

- 🚗 Traffic, Parking &amp; Transportation
- Where is Texas SandFest located?
- On the beach between Markers 13 and 17. North gate entrance: Marker 13. South gate entrance: Marker 17.
- Can we park our RV at Texas SandFest?
- Yes and no.
- Yes, outside the bollards south of Marker 19 (perpendicular parking only, max 3 nights). See City of Port Aransas RV parking regulations .
- No, inside the bollards, and no RVs, trailers, or oversized vehicles in SandFest parking lots. Texas SandFest assumes no liability for stuck vehicles, damage, or loss of property.
- Can I park my trailer, RV, or other oversized vehicle inside the general Texas SandFest beach parking lots?
- No, none are permitted in public SandFest beach parking lots. Park outside the bollards, per City rules (see above), or in the Gracies Way parking lot.
- Where can I park my trailer, RV, or other oversized vehicle?
- At Gracie&#39;s Way &nbsp;parking lot
- No overnight parking
- This parking lot is a field; park at your own risk.
- Can I park my RV on the beach outside Texas SandFest?
- Generally, yes on the west side of the bollards, north of Ave G or south of Marker 19, which is just south of Access Road 1A. No RV/trailer parking between Ave G and Marker 19 during the week leading up to the event and during the event. Obey all city ordinances and signage.
- Will traffic be one-way on the beach?
- Yes, typically southbound from ~9:00 AM to ~7:00 PM (Friday, Saturday &amp; Sunday) between Ave G and Access Road 1A. Both directions north of Ave G and south of Access Road 1A.
- SandFest Beach Access &amp; Parking The Best Route: 📍 Avenue G is your best option for accessing beach parking via vehicle or golf cart.
- Will I need a beach parking permit if I park in the Texas SandFest parking lots on the beach?
- Yes, it is required for any beach parking. More info here .
- Is there a cost to park?
- No, Texas SandFest does not charge for parking; the City requires a $12 parking permit (calendar year) for all beach parking. All three remote lots are free and do not require a parking permit.
- Where are the remote parking lots located?
- Gracie&#39;s Way :&nbsp; just off Access Road 1A (grass field)
- Roberts Point Park: 301 J.C. Barr Blvd.
- Port Aransas Community Park: 700 Clark Parkway
- Is overnight parking allowed in the parking lots?
- No overnight parking is permitted in any Texas SandFest lots, including Gracie&#39;s Way.
- Where is the bus parking?
- All bus parking (tour, school, charter) is at the remote lot at Gracie&#39;s Way &nbsp; (grass field)
- Will there be a golf cart shuttle to take me to and from my car?
- Yes and no. Yes, the shuttles will take you to the vicinity (generally ~1000 feet from where you parked), but not to your car. This applies to the main lot (inside bollards, ~Marker 12 to Ave G) and Gracie&#39;s Way . &nbsp;Priority: disabilities, injuries, elderly.
- What hours do the golf cart shuttles operate?
- 9:00 AM until 8:00 PM sharp each event day.
- Can we drive our golf carts inside the event?
- No, only official Texas SandFest golf carts allowed.
