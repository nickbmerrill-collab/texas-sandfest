Become a Volunteer! We love our Volunteers and are so greatful for your interest in becoming a Volunteer for Texas SandFest. Our success would not be possible without the time and support of our volunteers. General Volunteer Information ​ ​ ​ The minimum age to volunteer for all positions is 14. This event is outside, and almost all jobs will be in the sun and elements, including but not limited to heat, wind, and rain. The majority of jobs require standing and/ or walking for most of the shift. Most shifts are approximately 5.5 hours. Some jobs have a minimum age requirement and/or require a valid driver's license. Anyone driving equipment and/or a golf cart must be 25 years old​ Need assistance?
